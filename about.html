About Us

Welcome to RG Garments

where fashion meets exclusivity. Founded by Michael Gozie and Richard Botnaru, RG Garments is more than just an online clothing store; it's a destination for fashion enthusiasts and collectors seeking the extraordinary.

When you shop with RG Garments, you're not just browsing through products – you're embarking on a journey of discovery. Our sleek and user-friendly interface ensures effortless navigation, allowing you to explore our curated collection with ease.

At RG Garments,
we're passionate about providing a platform for rare and limited edition clothing items, ranging from shoes and jackets to trousers, jeans, and t-shirts. Our mission is to connect sellers of unique garments with discerning buyers who appreciate quality, craftsmanship, and individuality.
